About

Who I am

I'm a Full-Stack Developer and Machine Learning Engineer based in Myanmar. I hold a Bachelor of Computer Science with First Class Honours from STI Myanmar University in partnership with the University of Bedfordshire (UK).

I'm used to the intersection of practical AI and everyday software. I've built bilingual platforms that serve Myanmar users, voice assistants that run entirely on local hardware, and web systems that solve real institutional problems like timetable management for my own university.

I care deeply about offline-capable, privacy-respecting systems. Most of my AI projects use locally running models (Ollama, Whisper with CUDA) so they work without sending user data to the cloud which is important in a market with unreliable internet and limited cloud infrastructure.
